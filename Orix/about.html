About Us

Orix Furniture Uganda is the premier one stop center for office and domestic furniture,Curtains blinds and decorative accessories.
With 28 years experience, Orix Furniture Uganda has evolved into a fine tuned organization that understands the industry, the market and values its customers.
Orix Furniture Uganda is conveniently located in the city center of Kampala on Plot 41 Jinja Road.
Our showroom displays quality furniture and furnishing accessories.
The Showroom is air-conditioned and all floors are linked by a lift.
Customer parking is available on Jinja road.
